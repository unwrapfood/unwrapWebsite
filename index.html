Unwrap

Wrap the taste not just hunger

We are an Indian food tech company that is streamlining the way we order food online. Founded in 2021, we are a young company mobilizing technology to deliver world-class service to our customers. Facilitated by tech, we bring the reliability of a food brand with the ease of ordering online. We are providing one-hand solutions, from preparing fresh food to delivering on your doorstep through our online platform. We understand that every food business is built around taste, quality and trust, and we are adamant to provide it. We take care of every meal we deliver and also offer a wide range of food menu, including diabetic-friendly food to our customers. On the other side, we provide high-quality ingredients along with the recipes of our chefs.

Why Choose Us?

Making A Difference

We provide a wide range of food items, including a special diabetic-friendly menu to our health-conscious customers without compromising the quality taste of the food and focusing on delivering high-quality taste by keeping hygiene on track. We empower our customers to make a worth food choice.

Focusing on Affordability

Our users get customized diets, regular meal plans and a wide range of menu on a budget. We provide subscription plans and customized food delivery to our subscribed users at an affordable price to satisfy their food cravings and serve them the high-quality taste in the best possible way.

Accessibility

Being a food-tech company allows us to be available to our customers in the quickest and easiest way possible. Over time, our diabetic-friendly and fresh food deliveries reach more and more cities. We are actively expanding our menu and growing our services. We are continuing on the mission of delivering fresh and healthy Food to Feed India.

Maintaining the Quality

Maintaining quality is an ever going battle so here at Unwrap the process of ensuring quality beings by identifying a vendor. We assess vendors and helping them standardize to be able to provide quality food. Then we equip our vendors with set operating procedures and recipes developed by our own research and product department.
